Learn how to use RTB Document Planner / Wige gukoresha RTB Document Planner

Getting Started

1

Register Your Account

Click "Register" button on the home page. Fill in your name, phone number, email, and institution. Create a secure password and click "Register".

2

Login to Your Dashboard

Use your phone number and password to login. You'll be redirected to your personal dashboard where you can create documents.

3

Create Session Plan

Click "Session Plan" button. Follow the 4-step wizard: Basic Info, Course Details, Learning Content, and Review. Fill all required fields and click "Generate Document".

4

Create Scheme of Work

Click "Scheme of Work" button. Complete the 4-step wizard: Institution Info, Module Details, Term Content (3 terms), and Review. Generate your professional scheme.

5

Download Your Documents

After generation, click "Download DOCX" to save your professional RTB-compliant document. Documents are in Microsoft Word format ready to print or edit.

Intango

1

Iyandikishe Konti Yawe

Kanda buto ya "Register" ku rupapuro rw'itangiriro. Uzuza amazina yawe, nimero ya telefoni, imeri, n'ikigo. Kora ijambo ry'ibanga rikomeye ukande "Register".

2

Injira kuri Dashboard Yawe

Koresha nimero ya telefoni n'ijambo ry'ibanga kugira ngo winjire. Uzajyanwa ku rupapuro rwawe bwite aho ushobora gukora inyandiko.

3

Kora Session Plan

Kanda buto ya "Session Plan". Kurikiza intambwe 4: Amakuru y'ibanze, Ibisobanuro by'Isomo, Ibigize Kwiga, na Review. Uzuza ibisabwa byose ukande "Generate Document".

4

Kora Scheme of Work

Kanda buto ya "Scheme of Work". Soza intambwe 4: Amakuru y'Ikigo, Ibisobanuro bya Module, Ibigize Amaherezo 3, na Review. Kora scheme yawe yuzuye.

5

Pakurura Inyandiko Zawe

Nyuma yo gukora, kanda "Download DOCX" kugira ngo ubike inyandiko yawe yuzuye ikurikije amahame ya RTB. Inyandiko ziri muri format ya Microsoft Word ziteguye gucapwa cyangwa guhindurwa.
